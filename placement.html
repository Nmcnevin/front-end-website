Testimonials

Career & Placement Cell @ Marian is a portal to success for the aspiring students which helps them to imbibe the professional etiquettes integrated with appropriate attitude and aptitude through comprehensive pre-placement training sessions. If you know what you want and have the preparedness to go for it, Marian is there to help you to attain it. MCPC holds intensive career and placement training sessions, mock interviews, mock GDs etc. to sharpen your skills and equip you to meet the requirements of the recruiters. We provide the students the opportunity to make informed decisions about their choices and gain employability skills required for the fast changing technical world. Marian has two full time Placement Officers having a good understanding of the current trends in industry who are committed to helping students to build up their careers. They meet students to discuss employment strategies, help to develop in them a job aspiring attitude and devise grooming modules for them. It is a meticulous blend of career counselling and skill assessment along with spells of practice interviewing, soft skill development, resume preparations and interactive sessions with leaders of industry etc. Marian has an enterprising interface with top industrialists and successfully instituted solid linkages with reputed organisations around the globe for the benefit of her job aspirants. The Marian Alumni which is very influential with proven technical prowess also provides assistance to them to spot proper placements and internships. Marian has turned out to be the recruitment hub of Kerala in recent years and her departments could boast about their unmatched placement records. Marian also volunteers the unique ‘lifetime placement services’ which the students can make use of even after the completion of their courses. We also provide information and guidance for aspirants in higher studies, entrepreneurship/ self-employment and conduct coaching classes for exams like CAT, MAT, and GATE etc. It is a Marian characteristic that we render placement assistance for students with disabilities/ limited cognition, in linkage with non-profit organisations and Government agencies thereby helping them to come off their indispositions and pursue a dynamic and fruitful life.
